How to make a very simple egg and mayo sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Two slice of bread
  • 2 Egg
  • Mayonaise
  • Piece of butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Siracha

Instructions:

    1.Heat up a nonstick pan and drop in a piece of butter
    2.Crack the eggs and put them on the pan
    3.Use a spactula and move the eggs around until its like scrumble eggs
    4.Take two piece of bread and put Mayonaise on one side of each
    5.Put the eggs on top of one of the bread that has the Mayonaise
    6.Add some salt and pepper on top of the eggs
    7.Put the other piece of bread on top and enjoy
    Optional: Add some siracha on top of the eggs for the spicy
